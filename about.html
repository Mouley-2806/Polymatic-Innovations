1. Who We Are

At Polymatic Innovations, we bring together science, technology, and creativity to deliver advanced masterbatch and synthetic color solutions to industries across India and beyond. As trusted suppliers, we take pride in sourcing and providing high-quality masterbatch and colorants that enhance the performance, consistency, and appeal of every product they touch.

We specialize in supplying a comprehensive range of:

Every product we supply reflects our commitment to quality, consistency, and innovation.
