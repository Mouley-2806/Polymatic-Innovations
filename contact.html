Frequently Asked Questions

What are your typical lead times for masterbatches?

Lead times depend on order size and color complexity. Small runs (sample quantities) are usually ready within ready stock. Larger production batches typically take 5-7 business days. Please contact our sales team for a precise ETA.

Do you provide custom color matching services?

Yes. We offer custom color matching based on supplied samples, digital references, or product specifications. For best results, send a physical sample or a high-quality reference and we'll provide a profile and quotation.

What packaging options do you offer?

We provide FOC sample of 0.5-1 kg for trial.But our standard size of packaging for material supply is 25kg bag

Can you help with technical support and formulation?

Yes. Our technical team can advise on product selection, incorporation rates, compatibility, and troubleshooting. Provide details about your resin system and processing conditions for faster assistance.

How do I request a quote?

Use the contact details above or email Sales@polymaticmb.com with product details, quantities, and any specifications. We'll respond with pricing and lead time information.
